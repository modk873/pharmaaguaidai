Emergency Guide

Quick reference for emergency situations and first aid procedures.

🚨 Heart Attack

Chest pain, shortness of breath, nausea. Call emergency immediately.

🚨 Stroke

Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty. Act FAST.

⚠️ Breathing Difficulty

Sit upright, use inhaler if available, call emergency if severe.

⚠️ Severe Bleeding

Apply direct pressure, elevate if possible, call emergency.

ℹ️ Burns

Cool with running water, cover with sterile dressing, seek medical help.

ℹ️ Poisoning

Call poison control, do not induce vomiting unless instructed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app a substitute for medical advice?

No. This app provides educational information only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

How accurate is the drug information?

Our database includes common medications and their typical uses. However, medications change frequently, and individual responses vary. Verify with official sources.

Can I use this for emergency situations?

No. This app is not designed for emergencies. Call emergency services immediately for urgent medical situations.

Is my data stored securely?

Your notes and profile are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personal medical data to external servers.

How do I report an error in drug information?

Contact us through the Contact page with details about any inaccuracies. We continuously update our database based on feedback.

About PharmaCare

⚕️

Welcome to PharmaCare

PharmaCare is a modern healthcare companion designed to provide educational drug information, practical medical tools, and productivity features in a simple and accessible interface. The project aims to help students, healthcare professionals, and everyday users quickly access useful medical resources while maintaining an intuitive user experience.

🎯
Our Mission

Our mission is to make medical information and essential healthcare utilities easier to access through technology while encouraging responsible use and consultation with qualified healthcare professionals.

👁️
Project Vision

The long-term vision of PharmaCare is to become a comprehensive healthcare assistant that combines reliable educational content, smart productivity tools, medication management, and modern technology to improve access to medical knowledge.

✨
Key Features
  • Drug information library
  • Medical calculators
  • Medication notes and organization tools
  • Dark mode support
  • Multilingual interface
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Progressive Web App (PWA) support
  • Camera and barcode-related utilities where available
💻
Technologies Used

PharmaCare is built using modern web technologies, including:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • Local Storage APIs
  • Progressive Web App technologies
  • Responsive design principles
📚
Information Sources and References

The information presented in PharmaCare is compiled from educational materials, standard medical references, publicly available healthcare resources, and established clinical formulas where applicable. The project encourages verification through trusted healthcare organizations and official pharmaceutical references.

🤝
Open Source and Credits

This project acknowledges the contributions of the open-source community and the educational resources that make modern web development possible. Any third-party libraries, icons, fonts, or external assets should be credited according to their respective licenses.

⚕️
Medical Disclaimer

PharmaCare is intended for educational and informational purposes only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional healthcare advice. Users should always consult licensed physicians, pharmacists, or other qualified healthcare professionals before making medical decisions or taking medications.

🔒
Privacy

PharmaCare is designed to respect user privacy. Personal notes and preferences may be stored locally on the user's device. Users should avoid storing highly sensitive medical or personal information unless appropriate security measures are implemented.

⚠️
Accuracy and Limitations

Although every effort is made to improve the quality and reliability of the information provided, no guarantee is made regarding completeness or accuracy. Medical knowledge evolves continuously, and recommendations may change over time.
